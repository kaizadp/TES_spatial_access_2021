how much DOC was added as part of the amendment?

5 mL of 10.1 M acetate (CH3-COO-K)
1 mole acetate = 2 mole C

10.1 M acetate = 20.2 M C = (20.2 * 12) g/L C
5 mL of 10.1 M acetate = 0.005 L * 20.2 * 12 g/L C = 1.212 g C
